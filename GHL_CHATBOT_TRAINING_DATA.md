## 💉 SERVICES & PRICING

### 1. INJECTABLES

#### Botox / Dysport / Jeuveau
- **Price:** $12-14 per unit
- **Typical Units Needed:** 40-64 units for full upper face
- **Duration:** 3-4 months
- **Downtime:** None
- **Treatment Areas:**
  - Forehead lines
  - Crow's feet (around eyes)
  - Frown lines (between brows)
  - Bunny lines (nose)
  - Lip flip
  - Masseter (TMJ/jaw slimming)

**Common Questions:**
- "How many units do I need?" → Depends on area and muscle strength. Forehead: 10-20 units, Crow's feet: 12-24 units, Frown lines: 20-40 units. Book a consultation ($25, applied to your treatment) for exact quote.
- "Does it hurt?" → Minimal discomfort. We use ultra-fine needles and can apply numbing cream.
- "When will I see results?" → Initial results in 3-5 days, full results in 10-14 days.
- "Will I look frozen?" → No! We specialize in natural-looking results. You'll still have expression.

#### Dermal Fillers (Juvederm, Restylane)
- **Price:** $650-850 per syringe
- **Duration:** 6-18 months (varies by product and area)
- **Downtime:** Minimal (possible bruising/swelling)
- **Treatment Areas:**
  - Lips (enhancement, shape, symmetry)
  - Cheeks (volume restoration)
  - Under-eye hollows (tear troughs)
  - Nasolabial folds (smile lines)
  - Marionette lines
  - Jawline contouring
  - Chin augmentation

**Common Questions:**
- "How long do fillers last?" → 6-18 months depending on product, area, and metabolism.
- "Will my lips look fake?" → No! We focus on natural enhancement that complements your features.
- "Is there downtime?" → You may have some swelling and possible bruising for 3-7 days.

#### Kybella
- **Purpose:** Reduces submental fat (double chin)
- **Price:** Custom quote (typically $1,200-1,800 for series)
- **Sessions:** 2-4 treatments spaced 6-8 weeks apart
- **Results:** Permanent fat destruction

### 2. SKIN & LASER TREATMENTS

#### ClearLift "Lunchtime Laser"
- **Price:** $300-500 per session
- **Best For:** Maintenance, mild rejuvenation, prevention
- **Downtime:** ZERO - truly a "lunchtime" treatment
- **Sessions Recommended:** Series of 3-6 for best results
- **Results:** Gradual improvement in skin texture, tone, fine lines
- **Good For:** Busy people who can't take time off

**Common Questions:**
- "Does it hurt?" → Minimal sensation, no numbing needed.
- "Can I wear makeup after?" → Yes! Immediately after treatment.

#### CO2 CoolPeel Laser Resurfacing
- **Price:** $800-1,200 per session
- **Best For:** Dramatic results, wrinkles, sun damage, texture
- **Downtime:** 3-7 days of social downtime (red, peeling skin)
- **Sessions Recommended:** 1-2 for significant results
- **Results:** Significant improvement in wrinkles, scars, sun damage
- **The "Big Guns"** for anti-aging

**Common Questions:**
- "How long is recovery?" → 3-7 days. Days 1-3: Red like sunburn. Days 3-7: Peeling.
- "Will I see a big difference?" → Yes! This is our most transformative treatment.
- "Can I work during recovery?" → Many patients work from home. If you need to be in public, plan for 5-7 days.

#### IPL Photofacial
- **Price:** $350-500 per session
- **Best For:** Brown spots, sun spots, redness, rosacea, uneven tone
- **Downtime:** Minimal redness for a few hours
- **Sessions Recommended:** Series of 3-5
- **Results:** Clearer, more even skin tone

**Common Questions:**
- "Will it remove my sun spots?" → Yes! IPL targets pigment. Spots will darken and flake off.
- "Does it help with redness?" → Yes, excellent for rosacea and facial redness.

#### Microneedling
- **Price:** $400-600 per session
- **Best For:** Acne scars, texture, fine lines, collagen stimulation
- **Downtime:** 24-48 hours of redness (like sunburn)
- **Sessions Recommended:** Series of 3-6
- **Add-ons:** PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma) available for enhanced results

**Common Questions:**
- "Does it hurt?" → We apply numbing cream. Most patients describe it as mild discomfort.
- "Will it help my acne scars?" → Yes! Microneedling is excellent for acne scarring.

#### Chemical Peels
- **Price:** $150-300 per session
- **Types:** Superficial to medium depth available
- **Downtime:** Varies by peel strength (none to 5-7 days)
- **Results:** Brighter skin, improved texture, reduced fine lines

#### Dermaplaning
- **Price:** $95 (standalone) or add-on to facial
- **Benefits:** Removes peach fuzz and dead skin cells
- **Downtime:** None
- **Results:** Immediately smoother skin, makeup applies flawlessly
- **Frequency:** Every 3-4 weeks

#### GLP-1 Medications
**Semaglutide (Wegovy/Ozempic)** and **Tirzepatide (Mounjaro/Zepbound)**

**Program Includes:**
- Weekly injections (self-administered at home after training)
- Medical monitoring and check-ins
- Personalized nutrition guidance
- Monthly follow-up appointments
- Dosage adjustments as needed

**Pricing:**
- $400-600 per month
- **Membership Discount:** Beauty Bank members save $100/month

**Expected Results:**
- Most patients lose 15-20% of body weight over 12-18 months
- Average weight loss: 1-2 lbs per week
- Improved energy, reduced cravings, better blood sugar control

**Who Qualifies:**
- BMI ≥ 30 (or ≥ 27 with weight-related conditions)
- Medically appropriate candidates
- Commitment to lifestyle changes

**Common Questions:**
- "Is it safe?" → Yes! These are FDA-cleared medications with extensive clinical data.
- "What are side effects?" → Most common: nausea (usually temporary), decreased appetite, possible constipation.
- "Do I have to give myself shots?" → Yes, but we train you. It's a tiny needle in the belly fat - most patients say it's painless.
- "Will I gain it back if I stop?" → Weight maintenance requires ongoing lifestyle habits. We help you transition off medication when ready.
- "Is this the same as Ozempic?" → Semaglutide is the active ingredient in Ozempic/Wegovy. We use compounded versions from licensed pharmacies.

## ❓ COMMON QUESTIONS & ANSWERS

### General Questions

**Q: Is there a consultation fee?**  
A: Yes, the consultation fee is $25, which is applied toward your treatment when you move forward. This covers your personalized assessment, goal review, and Signature Transformation plan design.

**Q: How do I book an appointment?**  
A: Three ways: 1) Book online at our patient portal, 2) Call (858) 444-0414, 3) Use this chat widget

**Q: What's your cancellation policy?**  
A: We require 24-hour notice for cancellations. Late cancellations may incur a fee.

**Q: Do you take insurance?**  
A: Medical weight loss may be HSA/FSA eligible. Cosmetic treatments are not covered by insurance.

**Q: Is there parking?**  
A: Yes! Free parking at our Vista location.

**Q: What should I expect for my first visit?**  
A: Arrive 10 minutes early to complete paperwork. Your provider will discuss your goals, assess your skin/concerns, and create a personalized plan. No pressure - just honest recommendations.

### Treatment Questions

**Q: What's the best treatment for anti-aging?**  
A: It depends on your concerns and downtime tolerance. For zero downtime: ClearLift laser + Botox. For dramatic results: CO2 CoolPeel. Book a consultation for personalized recommendations.

**Q: How do I know which filler is right for me?**  
A: During your consultation, we'll discuss your goals and recommend the best product. Different fillers work best for different areas (lips vs cheeks vs under-eyes).

**Q: Can I get multiple treatments at once?**  
A: Yes! Many patients combine treatments (e.g., Botox + fillers, or laser + facial). We'll advise what's safe to combine.

**Q: How long do results last?**  
A: Varies by treatment:
- Botox: 3-4 months
- Fillers: 6-18 months
- Laser: Long-term with maintenance
- Weight loss: Permanent with lifestyle changes

**Q: Is there anything I should avoid before treatment?**  
A: For injectables: Avoid blood thinners (aspirin, ibuprofen), alcohol, and certain supplements for 24-48 hours to minimize bruising. We'll send you full prep instructions when you book.

**Q: When will I see results?**  
A: Depends on treatment:
- Botox: 3-14 days
- Fillers: Immediate (with final results in 2 weeks)
- Laser: Days to weeks depending on treatment
- Weight loss: Weeks 1-2 for appetite suppression, months for visible results

### Safety Questions

**Q: Are your providers licensed?**  
A: Yes! Our Medical Director is a board-certified MD. All injectors are board-certified RNs with specialized aesthetic training.

**Q: What if I don't like my results?**  
A: We specialize in natural results, so "overdone" is rare. Hyaluronic acid fillers can be dissolved if needed. We're committed to your satisfaction.

**Q: Are there any side effects?**  
A: Side effects vary by treatment. Common ones include temporary redness, swelling, bruising. Serious complications are rare. We discuss all risks during consultation.
