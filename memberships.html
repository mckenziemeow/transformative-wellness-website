FAQ

Membership Questions

How does the monthly credit work?

Your $149 Beauty Bank credit is automatically added to your account each month. Use it on any service — Edit, Concern Package, Method, IV therapy, anything. Credits roll over for up to 4 months (capped at $596), so you don't lose them if life gets busy.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Beauty Bank is month-to-month with no long-term commitment. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice.

Does Beauty Bank apply to the Concern Packages and The Method?

Yes. Your monthly credit applies toward any treatment on our menu — including Concern Packages and The Method. Many patients use Beauty Bank as a way to spread a Method investment across the year.

Is there a sign-up fee?

No. No sign-up fees, no annual fees, no hidden costs. Just your monthly membership.

What happened to Perks?

We retired the Perks tier in May 2026 to focus on making one membership genuinely worth it. Existing Perks members were contacted directly about their transition. If you have questions, contact us.

Can family members use my membership?

Membership benefits are for the primary member, but we offer family discounts — ask us about adding family members. Our Beauty Bank guest pass also gives you one complimentary Hydration Edit to share with someone each month.

Ready to Join?

Beauty Bank pays back the most for patients who already come in monthly. If that's you, we'd love to have you in the family.
