What to Expect

Before Your Treatment

  • Schedule your free skin consultation
  • Avoid sun exposure for 2 weeks before
  • Stop using retinoids 1 week prior
  • Arrive with clean, makeup-free skin
  • Discuss your skin goals and concerns

During Your Treatment

  • Skin analysis and treatment selection (15 min)
  • Pre-treatment prep and numbing if needed
  • Laser or treatment application (15-60 min)
  • Cooling and soothing post-treatment care
  • Aftercare instructions provided

After Your Treatment

  • Some redness/swelling is normal (varies by treatment)
  • Results timeline varies: immediate to 4-6 weeks
  • Protect skin with SPF 50+ daily
  • Follow post-care instructions carefully
  • Multiple sessions may be recommended
