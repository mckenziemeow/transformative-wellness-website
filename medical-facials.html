Medical Facials: Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between a medical facial and a regular spa facial?

A medical facial is performed in a medical spa by a licensed professional using medical-grade products and clinical techniques like professional extractions and microdermabrasion. Unlike a relaxation-only day-spa facial, it's built around your specific skin concerns and can be paired with treatments like lasers or microneedling for stronger, longer-lasting results.

What does microdermabrasion help with?

It gently exfoliates the outermost layer of dead skin to address dull texture, clogged pores, fine lines, and uneven tone, with no real downtime. It pairs well with a medical facial for an immediate glow.

How often should I get a medical facial?

For most people, every 4 to 6 weeks keeps skin clear, hydrated, and healthy: roughly your skin's natural renewal cycle. For a specific concern, we may suggest a short series first, then maintenance.

Is there any downtime?

No, our facials and microdermabrasion are no-downtime treatments. You may have mild, brief redness, but most clients head straight back to their day with SPF on.

Which facial is right for me?

That's what your consultation is for. We'll assess your skin and recommend the right facial, purifying, rejuvenating, or radiance, without pushing add-ons you don't need. You can also take our free AI skin assessment to start.
